About us

ACES stands for the Annual Conference for Engineering Students.
ACES is a leading student organization in the engineering community, it was first founded in 2004 in Faculty of Engineering Ain Shams University, to be the first student activity in governmental faculties in Egypt.

Mission

Since then, ACES has started its journey aiming to have a community of influencial leaders;
who can actually compete in the job market and stand out of the crowd by giving them the
opportunity to increase their capabilities technically as well as non- technically and unleash
their hidden potentials.

Vission

Doing our best to pay forward such a Life Changing Experience and have our impact on the surrounding socity hoping ACES journey will never come to end..
